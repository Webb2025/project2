What do we do?

We provide recipes and manuals on how to cook a dish that you can find via the search screen. There you can see every Recipe you need, Ingredients needed, where you can find or buy them (Address required), detailed instructions. If you want, you can get all the ingredients with an instruction manual sent to your house by pressing the delivery button at the bottom of the page. Lastly our members can submit and add new recipes or different versions of the same dish which will be verified by our team and added after the team assesses the validity of the submission. That way we can provide a diverse menu of dishes.
